10 career options after completing bachelor of architecture

There is a misconception about architecture that everyone has a similar job profile after completing his or her bachelor of architecture degree whereas the reality says quite otherwise but there are actually numerous career options after completing B.Tech Architecture. You must understand that if you are keen to pursue a career other than that of an architect, there are plenty of career options after completing B.Tech Architecture.
You can choose to either apply for jobs or go for higher studies in this field. There are plenty of career options after completing B.Tech Architecture, you just need to be qualified.

Architects

With a degree in bachelor of architecture, your first option is to become an architect by either opening your own firm or working with other experienced firms. You will have to do what you have done throughout your five years, design infrastructure as per client needs. The need for building infrastructure in never ending with constant development and so is the scope of this job profile.
Usually you can work straight as an architect after your graduation from any reputed college like SPA Delhi, SPA Bhopal, CEPT University , IIT kharagpur, etc.
You can get into these colleges through entrance exams such as JEE mains or NATA.
There are many known firms that you can apply to, while remuneration is important it is more essential to choose a firm according to their working style and culture for your growth in this field . Expected Salary - INR 4 lacs to INR 5 lacs per annum.

2.Architecture and engineering managers

With a certain knack of managing things, you are qualified for this job with a bachelor of architecture degree. Your role with this job profile will be to manage work between architects and clients in an architecture firm. You will plan and co- ordinate the activities of that firm. If you have been more into planning and teamwork in your college time than actually planning buildings, you should rather go for this job. With increase and expansion in the architecture industry there will be an increase in number of firms and hence an increase of architecture managers.
MBA or any online Architecture certification courses in business management will make your CV more eligible for this post.
You can apply to known firms like Oscar and Ponni architects, a Chennai based firm or Morphogenesis, a very well known Indian firm that has worked in many prestigious projects overseas.

3.Interior designer

One can easily opt for this job profile after bachelor of architecture and a certificate with interior architecture courses online will be an added benefit while applying for this job profile, you can even opt for M. Des. In interior architecture and then apply for this job. While as an architect you build spaces, as an interior designer, you design the interior of that space. Be it home, caf√©, restaurant, hospital etc. Interior designing gives life to that place so you can say interior designers are always in demand be it commercial buildings or residential.
The best interior designing forms to work with are SOBHA interior Bangalore, Ace interior Bangalore and Bonito Designs Bengaluru
Expected salary - INR 5-6 lacs per annum

4.Draftsmen

You can opt for this career after B.Tech Architecture after a bachelor of architecture or even a diploma in architecture , this job profile does not require you to be creative, you should just know how to make architecture drawings and you are ready for this job. Your role here is to come up with the final working drawings of a design by an architect that will go on the site for final construction.
You can work as a draftsperson if you have a really good command at CAD and other major architectural software's , you can enrol for an online architecture courses.
All known firms require draftsmen, for a well paying job choose a firm that has constant projects even if the form isn't big, best forms to apply to are Visionary Studio or Hi-Tech Cadd services.
Expected Salary: INR 2-3 lacs per annum

5.Landscape architect

You can choose this job profile after bachelor of architecture and an online certificate in landscape architecture or after post-graduation in this field. This is an evolving job profile since their job is to design open spaces like parks or outdoor spaces for institutes, industries and other infrastructure. There is a growing need for interesting outdoor spaces for people to get out of their homes, so this role may be soon in higher demand.
Best colleges for an M. Arch in landscape architecture are IIT Roorkee , JMI Delhi, etc. You can work as a landscape designer, consultant , architect after this course.
Firms like FICUS Landscape Architects and Design Cell are good picks to apply.
Expected Salary - INR 6 lacs per annum

6.Architectural historian

They usually work in the archaeological department as architectural historians and restorers, they study in detail about architecture and its history and various practices to conserve and restore historic monuments. You will most probably require a postgraduate degree for this. Best colleges are NIT Calicut and IIT kharagpur for an architecture degree related to this field.
You can even work as an architecture historian and preserver for monumental buildings as a government employee.
Expected salary - INR 6-7 lacs per Annum

7.Architecture journalists

Now they are the one working with magazines and other printing and informative industries who look over the architecture department, they have to constantly research and create content for their readers and viewers. A bachelor degree in architecture is enough with a good architecture courses. This is an interesting and evolving profession. Although there are no specific architecture journalism courses professionally, you can pursue an online architecture degree from prestigious universities in India as well as abroad to get an insight into the industry.
The architecture journalists can work with online publications, magazines and architecture-focused journals in India and abroad.
Architectural Digest and Idecorma are very famous magazines you can work with.
Expected Salary - INR 4-5 lacs per annum

8.Building Surveyor

As a building surveyor, you will have to survey and estimate the value of an infrastructure. It can be a commercial or a residential complex either for sale or for taking loan. This will require your deep knowledge in architecture engineering and surveyor license with your normal bachelor of architecture degree . As long as there is an infrastructure, the need to sell and finance that structure will rise.
You can work independently as a building surveyor or with any surveying firms like DTZ Madras.
Expected Salary - INR 5 lacs per Annum

9.Lecturer

You can work as a professor or a lecturer in any esteemed university after your graduation and post-graduation. Education is an over growing industry. For becoming a professor of architecture, it is suggested that you pursue a BTech architecture degree after which it is required for graduates to have at least 3 to 5 years‚Äô worth of experience.
As India is traversing a path towards more advanced infrastructural capabilities, having more and more experienced teachers who can help prepare the next set of brilliant architects is quite high, with universities perpetually recruiting lecturers in the architecture department.
With good qualifications from reputed colleges and certain experience you can work as a lecturer for top architecture colleges likeSir JJ college of architecture and CEPT University.
Expected salary - INR 4 to 5 lacs per annum

10.Graphic Designer

You can choose any software-oriented career after B.Tech Architecture as per your interest and talent; you will already learn to use this software in between your degree, with certain experience or some online course you can work as a professional in this field. There are multiple online, skill-specific courses as well as software architect courses that cover graphic design as an essential element or skill set. Pursuing them, you can easily land a good, high-paying job at a creative agency or large corporation and grow in your career after B.Tech Architecture further. While you can work flexible hours as a freelancer, you can also work with famous advertising agencies Like Buttercup advertising studio and Krish India Design as they pay really well to their graphic designers but acquiring a job there is quite difficult.
Expected salary - INR 3 to 4 lacs per annum
