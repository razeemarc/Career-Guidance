Career as Architect

A Bachelor of Architecture falls under the technical domain which solely focuses on the development of skills that are essential to being an Architect.

Duration

It is mandatory for candidates wanting to pursue this course to have a strong background in Science. Duration of the course might vary as per the country picked by the student, usually, the course is 5 years long but a few universities are offering a 3-4 year course in Bachelor of Architecture.

Bachelor of Architecture Subjects Covered

  • Art & Design
  • Building Sciences & Services
  • History of Architecture
  • Architectural Design
  • Building Construction
  • Drawing Communication
  • Theory of Structures
  • Workshop Practice
  • Computer & Software Lab
  • General Proficiency
  • Project Work
  • Design Applications

    Top Bachelor of Architecture Entrance Exams

    National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) NATA, one of India's most popular architecture tests, is held twice a year to determine eligibility for BArch admissions into universities. However, no joint counseling is provided, and individuals must apply to their favorite universities individually.
