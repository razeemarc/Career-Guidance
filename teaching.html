Upcoming Teacher Eligibility Tests (TETs)

eaching Eligibility tests are very important for teaching aspirants. CTET or Central Teacher Eligibility Test is the national eligibility test where candidates across the country can sit for the exam. There are other State TETs conducted by individual states and only candidates residents of that state can apply for the exam.
