Career as Teacher


The profession of teaching is one of the most dignified and reputed professions.The job of an educator is compelling as the knowledge given by them persists through ages and generations to come. The profession of teaching has plenty of scopes in India. From a play school teacher to a University lecturer, the profession of teaching is vast and employs a large number of people around the globe. From home tuitions to coaching classes, every field of imparting education is involved under the heading of teaching. Now, teacher have started choosing different teaching profiles like Education Consultant, Curriculum Developer, Career Counselor and Virtual Instructor leaving those classroom environment behind.

Lists of Teaching Exams in India are as follows

● CTET - Central Teacher Eligibility Test
● State TETs - State Teacher Eligibility Test (for each state)
● NET - National Eligibility Test(For Assistant professor & Junior Research Fellowship)
● PRT,TGT and PGT(For 1st to 12th class): Conducted By the KVS, DSSSB, APS & NVS

Skills required

  • Teaching as a profession demands you to be extremely careful of your personality, approach and knowledge
  • The teacher must be approachable enough for students to share any difficulties that they might face in their journey of learning
  • Teachers must be sensitive to the problems
  • Communication Skills- a teacher must necessarily have excellent communication skills for accurately conveying the knowledge to the students
  • Understanding Nature- this trait is mostly required for school level teachers
